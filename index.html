About Me

Hello, my name is Marta and I am a web developer in training, currently learning and developing my skills.

My interest in web development began in late 2019 when I took an intensive (bootcamp-like) course of basic full stack web programming. That sparked my desire to learn more about front-end development. I then decided to focus on this area and successfully completed a course in front-end development in December of 2020.

Since then, I have been honing my skills and expanding my knowledge in this field and I am eager to continue learning and growing as a developer.

In my spare time, I enjoy staying active by going to the gym, reading and listening to music.
